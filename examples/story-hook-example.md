# Story Hook Example

## Input
- Genre: sci-fi thriller
- Core idea: a delivery rider notices that every customer disappears the next day
- Main character: night-shift rider
- Conflict: he needs the money but the route feels cursed

## Example output
One-line hook:
Every night he delivered dinner to strangers. Every morning, one more address went dark.

Why it works:
- conflict appears early
- the visual is immediate
- the ending creates continuation pressure
