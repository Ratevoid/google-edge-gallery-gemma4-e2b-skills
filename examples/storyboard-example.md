# Storyboard Example

## Input
- Premise: A rider notices that every customer he serves disappears the next day.
- Main character: night-shift delivery rider
- Setting: rainy city at night
- Mood: tense and eerie
- Camera style: handheld with close-ups

## Example output summary
1. Rider checks a late-night order under a flickering streetlight.
2. He notices the address matches a building sealed yesterday.
3. He pauses outside the apartment door in the rain.
4. The door opens before he knocks.
5. Inside, the room looks abandoned except for a warm dinner table.
6. He looks up and sees himself already sitting there.

## Why it works
- each shot changes the situation
- the pacing is clear
- the ending creates a cliffhanger
